Author: Riya Patel
Title: The Joy of Morning Walks

There’s something magical about stepping outside just as the sun begins to rise. The air feels fresher, the streets are quieter, and the world seems to pause for a moment. Morning walks not only energize the body but also clear the mind, setting a positive tone for the rest of the day.

I often find inspiration during these walks — ideas for writing, solutions to problems, or simply gratitude for the little things around me. If you haven’t tried it yet, lace up your shoes tomorrow morning and take a short stroll. You might discover a new rhythm to your day.
