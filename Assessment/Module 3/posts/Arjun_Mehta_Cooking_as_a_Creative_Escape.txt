Author: Arjun Mehta
Title: Cooking as a Creative Escape

Whenever life feels overwhelming, I find myself drawn to the kitchen. Cooking is more than just preparing food — it’s a way to express creativity, experiment with flavors, and create something tangible that brings joy.

The process of chopping vegetables, mixing spices, and watching ingredients transform into a delicious dish is deeply therapeutic. It reminds me that small, mindful actions can lead to meaningful results. Sharing a meal with family or friends makes the experience even more rewarding.

If you’re looking for a simple way to unwind, try cooking a new recipe. You might discover that the kitchen is your canvas.
